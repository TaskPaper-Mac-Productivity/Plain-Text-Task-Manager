## What is TaskPaper?

![TaskPaper Interface](https://www.taskpaper.com/assets/img/lead.jpg)

TaskPaper is a professional-grade plain text editor specifically crafted for task management and project organization. It embraces the simplicity of text files while providing the structure and powerful tools needed to manage everything from daily checklists to large-scale creative endeavors.

Unlike complex project management software, TaskPaper gives you complete control through an intuitive syntax. Create projects by ending a line with a colon, add tasks with a hyphen, and assign customizable tags using the `@` symbol. This creates a system that is fast, flexible, and never locks your data into a proprietary format. Your tasks are stored in standard `.taskpaper` files, which are simple text files that can be opened anywhere, ensuring your work is accessible forever.

The application shines with its robust filtering and search capabilities. Instantly find all tasks tagged with `@important` or see what's `@due(today)`. Hide everything else to eliminate distractions and concentrate on the task at hand. It supports deep customization through themes and styles, allowing you to create a workspace that suits your visual preferences.

TaskPaper is designed for reliability and performance on modern systems, providing a seamless and responsive experience that keeps up with your most demanding workflows. It is the tool of choice for professionals who value simplicity, power, and longevity in their productivity systems.

## Tips & Tricks

- Use `@due(2024-12-31)` to add due dates to tasks
- Type in the filter bar to quickly find tasks by tag or project
- Use `Cmd+Option+]` and `Cmd+Option+[` to fold and unfold projects
- Customize your theme for a personalized look and feel
